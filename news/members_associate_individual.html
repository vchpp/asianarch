Susan L. Ivey

I am Director of Research and Evaluation at Health Research for Action, an affiliated research center of University of California, Berkeley. I am also Associate Professor in the Division of Community Health and Human Development, UC Berkeley School of Public Health. My research focus is health disparities research with a key interest in chronic disease risks in immigrant groups to the US, particularly Asian American groups. I have studied cardiovascular risks and diabetes in Chinese and South Asian populations in California. I am also a researcher with the CDC’s Healthy Aging Research Network and have examined the needs of older adults and caregivers in Chinese, Vietnamese, and Filipino populations in the SF Bay Area. (Click here for UCB Profile.)
Affiliated websites: Health Research for Action; Asian Diabetes
