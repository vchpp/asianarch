Cancer Control Award Contact Us! ☰

ARCH investigators offer research opportunities for interns to learn about qualitative and quantitative research methods and how to communicate and disseminate research. Interns will work collaboratively with fellow interns and multi-disciplinary researchers on research and projects focused Asian Americans and health disparities. Some tasks include assisting with data collection and/or management, preparing literature reviews, participating in weekly research meetings and co-authoring abstracts for submission to local and national conferences. Interns will also have opportunities to attend conferences and lectures.

If you are interested in becoming a research intern, please email Jennifer Fung (jennifer.fung2@ucsf.edu) with your CV/resume and cover letter.
